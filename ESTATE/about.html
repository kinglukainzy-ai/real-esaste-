Who We Are

Prodigal Real Estates was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide quality homes across Ghana. Initially focused on high-end residential properties and modern apartment developments, we quickly gained a reputation for professionalism and reliability.

As the company expanded, we recognized a critical need for affordable housing and committed to bridging the gap for middle- and lower-income families. This vision led to the creation of our catchphrase: “Homes for Everyone.”

Since our first project, Prodigal Real Estates has delivered over 500 housing units and continues to grow, with a strong commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.

Mission

To be a leader in providing quality, affordable, and modern housing solutions to our clients through a dedicated, skilled, and committed team, while maximizing value for all stakeholders.

Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative, high-quality, and affordable housing in Ghana, Africa, and beyond.
